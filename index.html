FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Do you offer financing on used motorcycles?

Yes—Shawnee Cycle provides financing on both new and used bikes with competitive rates and flexible terms. We work with various lenders to get you the best deal possible.

Where is Shawnee Cycle located?

We’re located at 16212 S Rogers Rd, Olathe, KS 66062—just minutes from downtown Kansas City. Visit us for a premium selection of powersports.

Absolutely—our service department will install any parts you supply, or you can shop our extensive range of OEM & aftermarket accessories in-store.

Click the “Schedule A Test Ride” button above, choose your model and preferred time, and we'll have it ready when you arrive. Valid license and gear required.
